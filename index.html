frequently asked questions

What is Acupuncture?

Acupuncture is a therapeutic modality of Traditional Chinese Medicine to promote natural healing and improve physical functions. It can be enhanced by applying heat or electrical stimulation at select acupuncture points.

Why Acupuncture?

Most of our patients have exhasuted all their treatment options including medication, physical therapy, chiropractic services, etc. Acupuncture is an effective, yet minimally invasive treatment sparing patients from reliance on drugs.

What to Expect?

Each treatment will take approximately an hour. The first will involve filling out forms and a consultation to discuss the problems you are having. The amount of treatments necessary for recovery depends on the severity and nature of the condition(s).

about us

Since 2001, Alex Lau Acupuncture is a family run business that has been serving the Bay Area for over 15 years to deliver professional
quality acupuncture services. Aculau Acupuncture will succeed and continue this tradition. We primarily treat physical injuries and bodily pain,
but are also able to address a wide array of ailments such as stress, colds, infertility, and many others.

David Lau, L.Ac.

David Lau is a Licensed Acupuncturist in the state of California. After completing his Masters at the University of East West Medicine in 2014, David has since worked closely with his father, Alex Lau and started his own acupuncture clinic.

Alex Lau, L.Ac.

Alex Lau is a board certified acupuncturist in the state of California with over 15 years of experience. He has treated patients of all kinds including those with back pain, muscle spasms, joint issues, stress, anxiety, fatigue, and sleep issues.
